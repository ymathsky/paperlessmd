1 System Overview

PaperlessMD is a browser-based Electronic Health Record (EHR) system built for Beyond Wound Care Inc. It replaces paper forms and clipboards with a mobile-friendly web application that works on any phone, tablet, or laptop — with offline support so field staff can continue working even when connectivity is temporarily lost.

📋

Digital Forms

10 clinical and consent forms with patient e-signature, ICD-10 search, and PDF export.

📅

Daily Schedule

Drag-sortable visit list per MA with status tracking and one-tap navigation.

📸

Wound Photos

Camera capture or file upload, grouped by wound site, with side-by-side comparison.

📏

Wound Measurements

Healing trend charts tracking width, depth, and granulation over time.

💊

Medication List

Master active medication list per patient, pre-filled on every visit consent form.

🗺️

Route Optimizer

Auto-sorts a day's visits by geographic proximity to minimize total drive time.

💬

Internal Messaging

Secure staff-to-staff messaging with multi-file attachments, drag-and-drop, and real-time unread badge.

🎤

Voice Dictation

Tap the mic icon on any text area to dictate notes hands-free.

💾

Auto-Save Drafts

Form progress saved every 15 seconds to localStorage — never lose a half-done form.

🔒

Every login, document view, and export is recorded with user + IP address.

2 Getting Started

2.1 Logging In

  • Open your browser and go to https://docs.md-officesupport.com
  • Enter your Username and Password. Use the eye icon to toggle password visibility.
  • Click Sign In. You will be redirected to your Dashboard.
⚠ Account must be active. If your account has been deactivated by an administrator, login will be refused even with correct credentials. Contact your admin.
Failed logins are recorded in the audit log. After too many failed attempts, contact your administrator.

2.2 Session Timeout

PaperlessMD automatically signs you out after 2 hours of inactivity. The timer resets on every page you visit. When the session expires you see a yellow banner:

"Your session expired. Please sign in again."

Simply log in again. Any forms you were filling out will be restored from the auto-save draft if they were saved within the last 24 hours.

5 Patients

5.1 Patient List

Click Patients in the nav bar. The list shows all patients, 20 per page.

  • Search: Type in the search box to filter by first name, last name, phone number, or date of birth.
  • All / Pending Upload tabs: "Pending Upload" shows only patients who have at least one signed form not yet uploaded.
  • Each patient row shows a count of their forms and photos.

5.2 Adding a Patient

  • On the Patients page, click Add Patient (top-right button).
  • Fill in the required fields: First Name, Last Name, Date of Birth, Address.
  • Optionally add phone number, insurance info, and emergency contact.
  • Click Save Patient. You are redirected to the new patient's record.

5.3 Patient View — Tabs

Click any patient name to open their record. The record has five tabs:

Last Visit Summary Card

At the top of the patient record (above the tabs) a summary strip shows the most recent visit: last visit date, the MA who attended, a vitals snapshot (BP / HR / O₂), and the number of forms submitted.

Required Forms Checklist

On the Forms tab, a checklist shows which forms are required for the current visit type. Forms already submitted on today's visit date show a green check ✅. Missing forms have a tappable link to open and complete them immediately.

5.4 Medications

The Medications tab shows two sections:

  • Active Medications — shown in sorted order. These are auto-filled into the Visit Consent form's medication list when the form is opened.
  • Discontinued — historical list, ordered by the date discontinued.

To add, edit, or discontinue a medication, use the controls on the Medications tab. Changes take effect on the next form that is opened for this patient.

5.5 Wound Measurements

On the Wounds tab you can:

  • Add a new measurement (length × width × depth in cm, plus granulation tissue %).
  • View a healing trend chart (Chart.js line chart over time).
  • Review the full measurement history table.

5.6 Wound Photos

On the Photos tab:

  • Photos are grouped by wound location (e.g., "Left heel", "Right ankle").
  • Click any photo for a full-screen lightbox view.
  • Compare mode: Select two photos from the same wound site and click Compare to view them side by side for healing assessment.
  • New photos are added via the Wound Care Photos form (see Forms section).

6 Forms

6.1 Available Forms

📋 Visit Consent (vital_cs)

Captures vitals (BP, HR, temp, weight, O₂ sat), race, chief complaint, homebound reason, and active medications. Requires patient signature. Pre-fills vitals and meds from the previous visit.

👤 New Patient Consent

Practice disclosure and consent for services. Required for every first-time patient.

📄 ABN (CMS-R-131)

Advance Beneficiary Notice. Notifies Medicare patients that a service may not be covered. Patient selects Option A, B, or C and signs.

📧 PF Portal Consent

Patient's consent to enroll in the Practice Fusion (PF) patient portal for electronic communication.

❤️ CCM Consent

Chronic Care Management enrollment consent. Required for CCM visit types.

🧠 Cognitive Wellness Exam

Multi-step cognitive assessment. Step 1 score auto-calculated (0–12). Results interpreted as: <7 impairment indicated, 7–10 more info needed, ≥11 no impairment.

🩺 Medicare AWV

Annual Wellness Visit health history. Includes the 15-question Geriatric Depression Scale (GDS) with auto-scoring.

📜 IL DHS Disclosure Auth.

Illinois Department of Human Services Authorization to Disclose Protected Health Information.

🩹 Wound Care Consent (wizard)

Three-step guided consent for wound care treatment. Step 1 covers general consent and risks (sections 1–6); Step 2 covers patient images, PHI use, and financial responsibility (sections 7–9) with nine individual acknowledgment checkboxes; Step 3 is the signature and submission. Includes an optional witness field.

📄 Informed Consent – Wound Care

Single-page version of the wound care informed consent matching the original BWC paper form. Patient Name and DOB are pre-filled. All nine consent sections are displayed as static read-only text. Patient or authorized representative signs at the bottom.

Note: All forms are blocked for the Billing role. Billing users can only view already-submitted forms.

6.2 Completing a Form

  • Open the patient record and go to the Forms tab.
  • Click the button for the form you need (e.g., Visit Consent).
  • Fill in all required fields. Fields marked with a red asterisk (*) are mandatory.
  • For ICD-10 codes, type at least 3 characters in the diagnosis search box. Results from the WHO ICD-10 API appear as you type — click to select.
  • Have the patient sign in the signature pad at the bottom.
  • Click Submit Form.

6.3 Auto-Save Draft

While filling a form, PaperlessMD automatically saves your progress every 15 seconds to the device's local storage (browser storage — not sent to the server yet).

  • A small dark pill badge appears in the bottom-right corner: "Draft saved".
  • If you close the tab, navigate away, or your session expires, reopen the same form for the same patient to see: "Draft restored (X min ago)".
  • Drafts expire automatically after 24 hours.
  • The draft is permanently cleared once you submit the form.
💡 Tip: The auto-save does not save the patient signature or CSRF token — the patient always needs to sign fresh on the final submission.

6.4 Voice-to-Text Dictation

Every text area on a form has a small microphone button (🎤) in its top-right corner.

  • Tap the mic icon on the field you want to dictate into.
  • The button turns red — speak clearly into your device microphone.
  • Your speech is transcribed and appended to any existing text in that field.
  • Tap the button again (or tap another field's mic) to stop recording.
Browser support: Voice dictation uses the Web Speech API, which is fully supported in Chrome and Edge. It is not available in Firefox. On unsupported browsers the mic button simply does not appear.
Dictation also works on text inputs named notes, description, or complaint.

6.5 Signing & Submitting

Every form requires a patient (or POA) signature before it can be submitted:

  • Scroll to the Signature section at the bottom of the form.
  • The patient draws their signature using a finger (on a touch screen) or mouse.
  • If a POA is signing, check the "Signing as POA" checkbox and fill in the POA name.
  • Click Clear Signature to redo if needed.
  • Click Submit Form. The form is saved with status Signed.

6.7 Exporting as PDF

Any signed or uploaded form can be exported as a printable PDF:

  • From the patient's Forms tab, click the PDF button next to the form.
  • A print-optimised page opens. Use your browser's Print (Ctrl+P / Cmd+P) and choose Save as PDF.
  • You can also export all forms for a visit date at once: on the Schedule page, click the Export PDF link next to a completed visit.
Every export is recorded in the Audit Log.

8 Schedule — Daily View

The Schedule page (accessible from the nav bar) shows all visits for one day. MAs see only their own visits. Admins can switch to any MA using the MA selector dropdown.

8.1 Navigating the Schedule

  • Click Today to jump back to the current date.
  • The date is also shown in the URL (?date=YYYY-MM-DD) — bookmark any date directly.
  • A stats strip shows counts for: Pending / En Route / Completed / Missed visits.

8.3 Google Maps Navigation

Each visit card shows the patient's address. Tapping Navigate (or marking a visit En Route) opens Google Maps with turn-by-turn directions to the patient's address.

  • On mobile, this opens the Google Maps app automatically.
  • On desktop, it opens in a new browser tab.

9 Admin — Manage Schedule

Accessible via Admin → Manage Schedule. Admin role only.

This page shows all visits for a selected date, grouped by Medical Assistant. Visits can be added, reordered, route-optimised, and deleted.

9.1 Adding a Visit

  • Select the Date using the date picker at the top.
  • Click Add Visit.
  • In the panel that appears, select: MA, Patient, Visit Time, Visit Type, and optional Notes.
  • Click Save. The visit is appended to that MA's list for the day.
Duplicate prevention: you cannot schedule the same MA to visit the same patient twice on the same day.

9.2 Reordering Visits (Drag & Drop)

  • Grab the drag handle (⠿ icon) on the left of any visit row.
  • Drag the row up or down to the desired position.
  • Click Save Order at the bottom of that MA's group to persist the change.

The new order is saved as visit_order and the MA's daily schedule page reflects it immediately.

9.3 Route Optimizer

When an MA has 2 or more visits, an ✨ Optimize Route button appears in that MA group's header.

  • Click ✨ Optimize Route.
  • The system geocodes each patient address using OpenStreetMap's Nominatim service (free, no API key). Progress is shown as "Geocoding 2/5…"
  • A nearest-neighbor algorithm (Travelling Salesman heuristic) calculates the order that minimizes total drive distance, starting from the first visit.
  • Visit rows are reordered with a green flash animation and the new order is automatically saved.
Rate limit: Nominatim allows one request per second. For a 5-visit route the optimization takes approximately 6 seconds. The button shows a progress counter during this time.
💡 Tip: After optimization, you can still manually drag-reorder individual visits if you need to override — for example, to put a time-sensitive visit first regardless of geography.

9.4 Deleting a Visit

  • Click the red 🗑 Delete icon on the right of a visit row.
  • A confirmation dialog appears. Click Confirm to proceed.
  • Deletion is permanent. If the visit was started (status not pending), consider marking it Missed from the schedule page instead.

10 Admin — Staff Management

Accessible via Admin → Users. Admin role only.

10.1 Viewing Staff

The users list shows all staff accounts with their role badge and active/inactive status. Sorted by role then name.

10.2 Adding a New Staff Member

  • Click Add Staff.
  • Enter Full Name and Username (required).
  • Select a Role:
    • MA — Medical Assistant (clinical access)
    • Billing — Billing only (no clinical data)
    • Admin — Full system access
  • Enter a Password (minimum 6 characters) and confirm it.
  • Click Save.

10.3 Editing a Staff Member

Click Edit next to any staff row. You can change the full name, username, role, and password. Leave the password fields blank to keep the existing password.

10.4 Activating / Deactivating

Click the Deactivate / Activate toggle on any staff row. Inactive staff cannot log in. Their historical records and forms are preserved.

You cannot deactivate your own account. Ensure at least one other admin account is active before deactivating yourself.

13 Offline Mode (PWA)

PaperlessMD is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Key pages and assets are cached by the browser's Service Worker so you can continue working with limited or no connectivity.

13.1 Installing on Your Device

  • Android/Chrome: A banner may appear "Add PaperlessMD to Home Screen". Alternatively, tap the browser menu ⋮ → Add to Home screen.
  • iOS/Safari: Tap the Share icon → Add to Home Screen.
  • Desktop Chrome/Edge: Click the install icon (⊕) in the address bar.

13.2 What Works Offline

  • Viewing previously cached pages (dashboard, patient list, schedule).
  • Filling forms — auto-save saves to localStorage on the device.
  • If you navigate to an uncached page, a branded offline page is shown with a "Retry" button.
Forms cannot be submitted offline. The final submit requires a live server connection. Draft data is preserved in auto-save storage until connectivity is restored.

14 Security & Privacy

ControlDetail
PasswordsStored as bcrypt hashes (PHP password_hash()). Never stored in plaintext.
Session securitySession ID regenerated on login. HTTPOnly, SameSite=Strict cookie flags.
CSRF protectionAll write operations require a CSRF token validated server-side via hash_equals().
Session timeoutRolling 2-hour inactivity timeout. Expired sessions are destroyed on next request.
Role enforcementPHP checks role on every request — there is no client-side role bypass.
Output encodingAll user-supplied data run through htmlspecialchars() before display (XSS prevention).
Audit trailAll logins, patient views, form views, and exports are logged with timestamp + IP.
HTTPSProduction runs over TLS (HTTPS). Credentials and PHI are never sent in plaintext.
Never share your login credentials. Each staff member must have their own account so the audit trail accurately reflects who accessed patient records.
Device security: Always log out when leaving a shared device. Do not use PaperlessMD on a public or untrusted computer. The 2-hour timeout is a safeguard but is not a substitute for manually logging out.

15 Troubleshooting

ProblemLikely CauseSolution
Login says "Invalid username or password" Wrong credentials or account deactivated Double-check username spelling. Contact admin if account may be deactivated.
Redirected to login with "session expired" message 2 hours of inactivity Log in again. Any draft forms will auto-restore on the form page.
Form won't submit — "CSRF token invalid" error Session expired between opening and submitting the form Do not leave the form open for hours without interaction. Log in again and the draft will restore.
Mic button not appearing on form fields Browser does not support Web Speech API Use Chrome or Edge. On Firefox, voice dictation is unavailable.
Draft not restoring after page reload Draft is older than 24 hours, or a different patient/form combination Drafts expire after 24h. Older data cannot be recovered from auto-save.
Route optimizer says "Geocoding failed" for some addresses Address not recognized by OpenStreetMap Nominatim Verify the patient address is complete and correctly formatted. Unrecognized addresses are placed at the end of the optimized route.
Photos not uploading / camera not opening Browser blocked camera permission Click the camera icon in the browser address bar and allow camera access. On iOS, grant permission in Settings → Safari → Camera.
Schedule page shows "Access denied" Logged in as Billing role The Schedule is not accessible to Billing users. Log in with an MA or Admin account.
Page loads blank / server error (500) Database migration not yet run on production Contact admin to run the pending migration scripts at /migrate_*.php.
Message attachment not showing after send File type not supported or file too large Check that the file is under 25 MB and is one of the supported types (JPG, PNG, PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, TXT, CSV, ZIP). An error alert will appear if rejected.
Conversation list shows "No conversations yet" after reload Browser was caching a stale API response Hard-reload the page (Ctrl+Shift+R). The Messages API sends no-cache headers — this should be a one-time issue on older cached versions.
Offline page showing even with internet Stale Service Worker cache Open browser DevTools → Application → Service Workers → click "Update", then hard-reload (Ctrl+Shift+R).

16 Quick-Reference Cheat Sheet

Daily MA Workflow

  1. Log in → Dashboard shows today's schedule and any pending drafts.
  2. Go to Schedule to see the full day's visit list in order.
  3. Tap a visit → tap Navigate to open Google Maps.
  4. Tap En Route when leaving for the patient.
  5. On arrival, tap the patient name to open their record.
  6. Complete required forms (check the Required Forms Checklist on the Forms tab).
  7. Patient signs each form → tap Submit Form.
  8. Add wound photos via the Wound Care Photos form if applicable.
  9. Update wound measurements on the Wounds tab.
  10. Mark visit Completed on the Schedule page.

Daily Admin Workflow

  1. Log in → check Dashboard for pending signed forms and today's stats.
  2. Go to Admin → Manage Schedule to set up the next day's visits.
  3. Add visits for each MA, assign visit type, set approximate times.
  4. Run ✨ Optimize Route per MA group to minimize drive time.
  5. Review any MA's daily progress via Schedule → MA dropdown.
  6. Export PDFs for completed visits for billing upload.
